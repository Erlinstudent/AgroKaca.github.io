How We Help

Services That Go Beyond the Sale

We don't just sell products — we provide the knowledge and support to make them work. From your first soil test to your final harvest.

Agricultural Consultation

Our expert agronomists work directly with you to understand your land, your goals, and your budget. We then design a tailored programme covering everything from soil preparation to harvest timing.

Whether you're a small family farm or a larger operation, we provide honest, practical advice with no hidden costs or unnecessary upselling.

  • Initial farm visit and assessment
  • Personalised crop and input planning
  • Seasonal follow-up check-ins
  • Problem diagnosis and troubleshooting
  • First consultation always free
🧪

Soil Analysis

Understanding your soil is the foundation of productive farming. We collect samples from your fields and send them to a certified laboratory to measure pH, macronutrients (N, P, K), micronutrients, and organic matter content.

You receive a clear, readable report with specific fertilizer and amendment recommendations based on your actual soil data — not guesswork.

  • Field sampling and preparation
  • Laboratory pH and nutrient analysis
  • Organic matter and texture assessment
  • Written recommendations report
  • Follow-up interpretation session
📋

Crop Planning & Rotation

Poor rotation is one of the biggest causes of yield decline, disease build-up, and wasted inputs. We help you plan a multi-year crop rotation that breaks pest and disease cycles, maintains soil health, and maximises income.

We take into account your available equipment, market access, irrigation, and labour constraints to build a plan that actually works on your farm.

  • 2–4 year rotation design
  • Variety selection per crop and zone
  • Planting and harvest calendar
  • Input budget estimation
  • Annual review and adjustment
🔍

Pest & Disease Diagnosis

Spotted something unusual on your crops? Send us photos or bring samples to our shop and we'll identify the issue and recommend the correct treatment within 24 hours.

Fast, accurate diagnosis means less product wasted on the wrong treatment and less time lost to spreading problems.

  • Photo-based remote diagnosis
  • In-store sample analysis
  • Field visit for complex cases
  • Specific product recommendation
  • Application method guidance
🚚

Delivery Across Albania

We deliver all products directly to your farm or collection point. Orders placed before noon are dispatched the same day for standard areas. We handle bulk orders for cooperatives and large farms with special logistics support.

  • Same-day dispatch for in-stock items
  • Delivery across all Albanian regions
  • Bulk and pallet orders welcome
  • Cooperative group orders with discount
  • Returns and exchange policy
