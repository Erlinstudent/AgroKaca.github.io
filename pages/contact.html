Common Questions

Quick Answers

Do you deliver across all of Albania? +

Yes. We deliver to all regions of Albania. Orders placed before 12:00 are dispatched the same day for areas near Durrës. Remote areas may take 1–2 additional days.

Are your products certified for use in Albania? +

Yes. Every product we sell holds the required Albanian plant protection registration. We only stock products that are legal, labelled, and safe for use under Albanian regulations.

How long does a soil analysis take? +

Standard turnaround is 5–7 business days from when we receive your sample. We'll contact you as soon as results are ready and book a call to walk you through the recommendations.

Can I return a product I haven't used? +

Yes, unopened products in original packaging can be returned within 14 days of purchase. Contact us first and we'll arrange collection or you can return in-store.

Do you work with agricultural cooperatives? +

Absolutely. We offer volume pricing and dedicated support for cooperatives and group buying schemes. Get in touch to discuss your needs and we'll put together a tailored proposal.
